About the meeting

Welcome to the International Conference on Art History

The International Conference on Art History brings together international research and critical debates on art, art history and visual culture. This important international conference on art history is an opportunity to learn about the latest research, hear major keynote speeches, broaden networks and exchange ideas.

Open to all, the annual conference attracts about 200 attendees each year and is popular with academics, curators, practitioners, phD students, early career researchers and non-art historians working on related research.

Although the 2023 Annual Meeting was considered a face-to-face event, we made the difficult decision to move to a fully digital platform due to the uncertainty of the evolving Covid-19 restrictions and ensuring the safety of delegates and staff. As a result, all aspects of the conference will be available digitally during the three-day event.
